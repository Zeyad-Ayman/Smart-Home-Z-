General Note:   #Capital Letters are for "ON", and Small Letters are for "OFF" !!

Instructions:
-----------------------
'A' ===> Room A  Lights ON
'a' ===> Room A  Lights OFF

'B' ===> Room B  Lights ON
'b' ===> Room B  Lights OFF

'C' ===> Room C  Lights ON
'c' ===> Room C  Lights OFF

'T' ===> A.C. Sys. ON, Setting Temp.
't' ===> A.C. Sys. OFF

'F' ===> A.C. Fan ON
'f' ===> A.C. Fan OFF
